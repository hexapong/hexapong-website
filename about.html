About:
Project “Hexapong“ started at the Code+Design Camp 2017 in Cologne
which took place from 15th to 18th june in 2017
and brings designer and coder together in order to realize some great projects like this.
The idea comes from one of the first games for computers called “PONG“.
We wanted to extend the game and during the brainstorming the idea of a multiplayer-Pong for six people came up.
The game should be realized in a hexagon and just like in Pong the goal of the game is to get the ball in the enemies goal.
But there’s not just one enemy you have to play against but five who all want to be the one that survives til the end to earn one magical point.
